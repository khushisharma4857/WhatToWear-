# WhatToWear - User Personas

## Persona 1: Emma - The Busy Professional

### Demographics
- **Age**: 28-35
- **Gender**: Female
- **Occupation**: Corporate professional (marketing, finance, tech)
- **Income**: $75K-$120K
- **Location**: Urban/Metropolitan areas
- **Device**: iPhone 14 Pro

### Characteristics
- Works 45+ hours per week
- Commutes to office 3-4 days/week
- Values time-saving solutions
- Style-conscious but time-poor
- Tech-savvy and early adopter
- Active on Instagram
- Budget-conscious but willing to invest in quality

### Goals
- Reduce morning decision fatigue
- Look polished for work
- Maintain professional appearance
- Build versatile wardrobe
- Save time on outfit selection

### Pain Points
- Wastes 20-30 minutes deciding what to wear daily
- Forgets about items in the back of closet
- Buys similar items repeatedly
- Struggles with work-appropriate outfit combinations
- Too busy to organize wardrobe

### WhatToWear Usage
- Opens app 5-6 days/week
- Uses AI Assistant "What should I wear today?"
- Saves favorite work outfits
- Uses app 10-15 minutes per session
- Shares outfits on Instagram stories occasionally

### Key Needs
- **Speed**: Quick outfit recommendations
- **Professionalism**: Office-appropriate suggestions
- **Simplicity**: One-tap solutions
- **Reliability**: Consistent quality recommendations

## User Journey Maps

### Emma's Day (The Busy Professional)

**6:45 AM** - Alarm goes off, rushes to get ready
- Opens WhatToWear app
- Asks: "Professional outfit for office meeting"
- Receives 3 suggestions in 10 seconds
- Selects outfit, gets dressed
- **Time saved**: 15 minutes

**Touchpoints**: App notification at 6:30 AM, quick recommendation, confidence boost

### James's Week (The Style Enthusiast)

**Monday** - Browses public outfit feed
- Discovers trending outfit combination
- Saves to "Inspiration" collection
- Checks if he owns similar items

**Wednesday** - Creates new outfit
- Uses outfit builder
- Remixes saved inspiration
- Shares on Instagram
- Receives comments and likes

**Friday** - Engages with community
- Comments on friend's outfit
- Gets styled by AI assistant
- Plans weekend outfit

**Touchpoints**: Feed discovery, creation, sharing, community engagement

### Sarah's Organization Day

**Saturday Morning** - Seasonal wardrobe purge
- Opens app to view all winter items
- Tags items for donation
- Plans outfits for upcoming week
- Checks what needs repairs

**Planning**: Uses calendar view for upcoming week
- Pre-plans outfits for each day
- Notes weather preferences
- Ensures variety and cleanliness

**Touchpoints**: Organization, planning, maintenance, efficiency

## User Research Insights

### Quantitative
- 73% of users waste 20+ minutes daily on outfit selection
- 68% of wardrobes are only 20% actively worn
- 81% interested in AI-powered style recommendations
- 55% would share outfits with friends
- 42% would pay for premium features

### Qualitative
- "I keep buying the same thing because I forget what I own"
- "I want to feel confident in my outfit choices"
- "Show me combinations I wouldn't think of myself"
- "I need this to work without thinking about it"
- "Make me feel stylish without effort"
